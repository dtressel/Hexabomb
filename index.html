How To Play

Hexabomb is a play on the original Minesweeper. However, unlike Minesweeper where you avoid the bombs, in Hexabomb, you need to find and disarm the bombs before they explode! When you start a game, bombs are randomly placed and hidden under any of the hexagonal tiles. A list of bombs and the time (number of turns) until they explode are displayed on the right.

Click on a tile to reveal either a bomb (yellow tile) or a number showing how many tiles away the nearest active bomb is. If a red number is revealed that means that the nearest active bomb is also the bomb that is next to explode! If multiple active bombs are equidistant from a tile, and one of the bombs is the next bomb, the number will appear red.

Your first task is to find some tiles with red numbers to find the first bomb before it explodes. Once you find the first bomb, the numbers on the revealed tiles will update to reflect its distance from the nearest active bomb. Keep using the red numbers to triangulate and find all the bombs!
